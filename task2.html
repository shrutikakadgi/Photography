Hi there! My name's John and I'm a lifestyle photographer in Fort Wayne, Indiana. I've been
practicing photography for the past 12 years and have done it professionally for 4 years
now. Photography is my biggest passionin life- capturing the raw and, real moments.. the
genuine laughter and true love is what I strive for. Outside of photography I'm a mom and
bonus mom of two beautiful boys, an at-home musician, and I spend my days laughing with
the father of my children and best friend! I'm originally from Austin, Texas and am still a
very proud Texan at heart! Sessions with me are so fun - you'll be moving, laughing, full of
love and will leave being so glad you did this! If you have any questions, don't hesitate to
reach out, and I look forward to meeting you!
